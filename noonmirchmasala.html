About Us

At Knife and Fork Cafe , where traditional dishes from all regions of India are served, you will find distinctive, savory food that will tickle the taste buds and delight the belly. Some of the Indian fusion dishes, like mango and coconut chicken curry, are to die for and you will not find anywhere else. You have the choice of either choosing to come down to our fine establishment and dine here, or you can order the food with a press of a finger and dine in the comfort of your own home.

Getting a Table

Here at Knife and Fork cafe, getting a table cannot ge any more simplier than this. All you really have to do is come and and tell our lovely waitresses how many people are dining today and they will direct you to the first available table.
