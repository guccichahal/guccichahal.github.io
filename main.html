Welcome To Enver Creek Secondary

Enver Creek Secondary is a public school in Surrey School district SD36. At enver Creek, we teach the required acedemic courses and several other extra curricular activites as well as several sports teams and afterschool clubs.The administration here at Enver Creek Secondary consists of the Principal, Mr. Kilpatrick, and the two Vice Principals, Mr. Magnessun, and Ms. Sadhra.
